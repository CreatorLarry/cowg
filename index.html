Who We Are

The Center For Water Governance (CEWAGO), is an NGO that Educates & Empowers communities in issues of WASH (Water, Sanitation & Health). The education involves Re-using, Reducing & Recycling of waste.

The target communities are those living in marginalised areas/slums & in the rural areas where there is little or no access to clean water & sanitation facilities

The main objective is, To improve access to water for poor communities in order to accelerate achievement of MDG's.
